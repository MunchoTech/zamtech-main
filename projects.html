System 2025-2026

Lusaka Digital Readiness Index (LDRI)

A structured evaluation framework designed to assess how well digital platforms in Zambia serve local users, particularly non-technical users and speakers of Nyanja and Bemba.

The Three Dimensions of Audit

🗣️

Linguistic Accessibility

Assessing terminology clarity and navigation ease for users who primarily speak local languages.

🛑

User Friction

Identifying where users hesitate or abandon tasks due to complex logic or poor error recovery.

📊

Data Representation

Ensuring system logic reflects real Zambian behaviors and informal user patterns.

Framework Definition

The LDRI IS:

  • Research-based audit tool
  • Decision-support system
  • Standardized framework

The LDRI IS NOT:

  • A commercial software product
  • A marketing scorecard
  • A ranking or shaming tool

2025-2026 Deliverables

Finalized Framework • Validated Methodology • 5 Completed Audits • Consolidated Findings Report
